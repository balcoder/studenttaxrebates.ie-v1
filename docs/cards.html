Part-Time Accounts & Payroll

Fcos Ltd is an innovative and progressive Irish owned company based at Cork Airport Business Park. We are currently recruiting a Payroll/Accounts Administrator to join its busy Payroll and Accounting team.

This role is flexible can be Part-Time or Full Time

Ideal candidate should be a recent Ipass graduate. Experience in payroll not essential as full training will be provided.

Main duties of the role:

  • Prepare weekly payroll for up to 20 clients.
  • Prepare weekly payroll for up to 20 clients.
  • Prepare P30’s/P35’s/P45’s etc
  • Administer third party deductions – Healthcare, Unions, Pensions.
  • Analyse and report payroll costs to management.
  • Scanning and matching of invoices
  • Posting invoices into Exchequer
  • Bank Reconciliation

Requirements:

  • Must be a self starter – capable of working on your own initiative
  • Must be interested in pursuing a career in payroll/Accounting
  • Proficient in the use of Microsoft Excel
  • Excellent attention to detail to ensure every employee is paid correctly and on time.
  • Excellent customer service skills.

Accounts Administrator

Fcos are recruiting for a Junior Accounts Administrator to support our ever expanding accounting teams here in Cork Airport Business Park. We are looking for someone with good IT skills, who is organized - with good attention to detail.

The main duties of the role are:

  • Sorting and coding of all packs from the retailer.
  • Scanning and organizing of invoices.
  • Matching of invoices - ensuring that there is record of any missing invoices or dockets.

This role does not require previous experience - just a willingness to learn.

Full training will be provided.

Job Type: Full-time
Salary: €20,000.00 to €21,000.00 /year

Trainee Accountant

Fcos are currently recruiting for enthusiastic and ambitious Trainee Accountant to join our ever expanding team here in Cork Airport Business Park. This is a 3 year training contract.

Ideally we are looking for Business Graduates with a Level 8 degree who want to go on and qualify as accountants. Fcos offer full support to students in their ACCA/CPA exams.

We provide exam support, study leave and are a Gold Approved Employer for ACCA.

What can Fcos offer you?

  • High reputation as an in-house training firm
  • Opportunity for varied industry training
  • Continual assessment and feedback
  • Genuine opportunity for career progression
  • Active social club
  • Friendly and Supportive environment

We offer experience in the following areas:

  • Invoice processing
  • Monthly creditor reconciliationsContinue
  • Issuing supplier payments
  • General ledger maintenance
  • Bank Reconciliations
  • Weekly controls
  • Weekly reporting to client (KPI)
  • Periodic statutory returns (Vat, Environmental Levy)
  • Assisting account manager with preparation of quarterly management accounts

Job Type: Full-time
Salary: €20,000.00 to €22,000.00 /year

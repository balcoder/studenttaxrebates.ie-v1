About FCOS Ltd.

Fcos is an innovative Irish company providing financial control outsourcing services to over 50 SuperValu and Centra retailers nationwide. Founded in 2004, Fcos has earned the reputation for excellence in the services we provide. Fcos has invested in the areas of staff training & development and has assembled an ambitious team of professional staff and we are now ideally placed to expand our client base.

Letter from Managing Director

Fcos has changed the way of doing business for SuperValu and Centra retailers. In 2004 we identified the need for our service and developed a top class product. Since then we have exceeded our client expectations, we will continue to innovate and improve. At Fcos we have built an incredible team of people, many of whom are with us since our formation. We are now well placed to expand our client base, whilst continuing to deliver our high standards. If Fcos can add value to your business, I would be delighted to hear from you. Diarmuid Coakley FCA., Managing Director, Fcos.
